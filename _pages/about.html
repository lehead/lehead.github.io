Louise Head

{% include post_picwrap.html pos = "left" max = "256" src="/images/louise_hair_edit.jpg" %} Louise is a queer, femme coach and writer who works with relationships that need help overcoming recurrent points of tension and with individuals who need help reconnecting with their sense of purpose.

Louise has been featured in Harper's Bazaar, Bustle, Essence, and Modern Fertility.

Louise received her Bachelor’s degree from Tufts University, her Master’s degree in Marriage and Family Therapy from the California School of Professional Psychology, and is a sex educator certified by San Francisco Sex Information.
